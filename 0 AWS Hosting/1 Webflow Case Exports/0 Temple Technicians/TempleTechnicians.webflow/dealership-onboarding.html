Revenue Sharing

  • A portion of the sale goes directly to you, the dealership or individual salesman. Amount varies by system, click on "Amount Earned" for full details.
  • Services are sold at my prices / MSRP.
  • Clients can expect an install typically within a 5 business day period.
  • Since you are getting cash back or a kick back, this avenue may impact how you file your taxes. It would require a W9 form, and then I would issue a 1099-NEC at year's end if the amount sent to you is greater than $600.

    EXCEPTION: If your business entity is a corporation, then you would simply report this income on your 1120 or 1120S Form (your schedule-C or your K-1 respectively).

Recommended for:
‍
Dealerships or salesman who wish to produce a payout for each successful remote start sale. There is no cost involved in this scenario, simply steering and closing sales at full MSRP (as provided by me) lands a commission. The client does not receive any discount, but the dealership makes a profit.

White Label Service

  • You would sell the remote starts as your own service, then I would fulfill the order for you.
  • Prices are set by you, but must meet a minimum (otherwise none of us will make money). [Market Research as well as recommended prices are included below].
  • Clients can expect an install typically within a 5 business day period.
  • In this scenario, I would be considered an independent contractor and you would need to issue a 1099-NEC to me at year's end. I would include my own W9 so long as payments to me for fulfilling the white label services are greater than $600.
  • Insurance and warranty would run under my business to provide a worry-free sales experience, you would just need to make the sale and hand the client off to me.

Recommended for:

Dealerships that want to manage their own pricing and offer premium remote start systems under their own brand. No need to hire staff, worry about adding insurance, or worry about warranty work. Simply sell the remote starts at a price point your client base falls into and then take a larger piece of the sale home. See recommended sell ranges for each system below.

Vouchers

  • This would be a pre-paid package of either 3, 5, or 7 units which provides the deepest discounts available + shatters the market rate.
  • Services would be expedited to have the remote start installed at the next available slot, typically less than 5 business days.
  • Concierge service included as optional. I will drive to your dealership lot OR the client's house to pick up their vehicle and then drive to my shop with their vehicle to perform the install. Then I would return the vehicle to the lot or the client's house [limit 15 miles].
  • With vouchers, you have the flexibility to do with them as you see fit allowing you for maximum flexibility. You can purchase these vouchers and then still sell the systems at white label pricing OR you can simply use the vouchers to provide deep discounts to your client's, family members, friends, etc.
  • Insurance and warranty would run under my business to provide a worry-free sales experience, you would just need to make the sale and hand the client off to me.

Recommended for:

Any dealership, individual, or who is looking at maximizing their value. These vouchers are available if you want to simply buy a 3-pack for your friends and family or to have on hand for your dealership client base. Setting a base profit margin without markup on goods or services while providing an optional concierge service backed by a limited lifetime warranty (plus 1-3 product warranty) is the best offer on market to date, anywhere.

Exceptions

  • Audi, BMW, Volkswagen, Mercedes-Benz, and other luxury brand vehicles are set a separate pricing. Talk to me for more details.
  • I hold the right to refuse any service on any vehicle at any time. I basically do any install that is 2010+ and I avoid any installs that require inline resistors, diodes, or external relays since these components have a higher failure rate than any other component in the system and they can lead to poor reliability.
  • I am building a list of accepted vehicles such that anyone who is interested can simply lookup their vehicle for verification.
