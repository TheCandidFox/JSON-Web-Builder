Base

$275
Base installation

This is a straightforward, no-frills remote start system. It’s a 1-Way setup, meaning there’s no confirmation signal sent back to your remote—you’ll need to visually check your car to ensure it’s running.

Recommended for short distances where you can see your car. This system is perfect for starting your vehicle from your kitchen to your driveway. It will struggle with longer distances and getting the signal through multiple walls, but typically has enough range for an entry-level system. It can be upgraded to a 2-Way system later if you decide you want more range.

This will include:

  • DC3 Control Module
  • Wires for installation
  • (2x) 1-Button R2 Remotes | 1,500 FT Range (Line of Sight)

Drone Mobile

This system builds on the Base Installation, adding the convenience of a Drone Mobile Telematics module. With this upgrade, you can start, lock, or unlock your vehicle using your smartphone from virtually anywhere—as long as both you and your car have cell reception. Perfect for travelers or anyone wanting maximum control at their fingertips.

Recommended for people who want 2-Way communication, unlimited range, and an approximate GPS location (displayed in-app) with 1-Way remotes as backups or spares.

Prime

$350
Prime System

Everything in the Base Installation, plus a 2-Way remote (R2 or G15—check out the video for more info). With this upgrade, you’ll hear chimes from your remote to confirm your car has started, adding convenience and peace of mind.

Recommended for anyone wanting 2-Way communication without committing to a Drone Mobile subscription. This system offers ample range—typically enough to start your car from a movie theater seat during the credits, provided the key fob batteries are at full charge and you are not in a parking garage (open lot layout).
